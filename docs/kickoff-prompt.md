## Mission & thesis
Turn open educational resources into a navigable PREREQUISITE GRAPH of one-concept-at-a-time lessons, with a DETERMINISTIC pathfinder routing a learner from what they know to a goal. It is NOT a chatbot. The thesis is load-bearing and governs every decision:

**The AI is allowed to BUILD the map; the map is not allowed to TRUST the AI.**

A GPT-5.x call atomizes OER into the graph AT BUILD TIME. A deterministic walk reasons over the committed graph at request time with **NO LLM on the request path, ever.** Every gate below exists to stop a non-deterministic build step from producing a graph the deterministic core silently trusts.
